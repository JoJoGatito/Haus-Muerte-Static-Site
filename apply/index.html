Frequently Asked Questions

What types of performers do you accept?

We welcome applications from all types of performers including musicians, comedians, dancers, theater artists, poets, and more. We're always looking for unique and engaging performances that will resonate with our audiences.

How long does the application process take?

Our curation team reviews applications on a rolling basis. You can expect to hear back from us within 2-3 weeks after submission. If your application is successful, we'll contact you to discuss potential performance dates and details.

Do you provide equipment for performers?

We provide standard sound equipment, lighting, and basic stage setups. For specialized equipment, please indicate your requirements in the application form. We'll discuss specifics with you if your application is successful.

How are performers compensated?

All performers receive compensation based on factors including experience level, audience size, and event type. We believe in fair pay for artists and will discuss specific rates during the booking process.

Can I apply if I don't have professional experience?

Yes! We value talent and originality over professional experience. New performers are encouraged to apply. Please include any samples of your work, even if they're informal recordings or videos.
