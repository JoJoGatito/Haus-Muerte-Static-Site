Performance Opportunities

We provide a space for disabled, queer, and POC folk to perform in whatever way they see fit. We will accomodate your needs and let you be as weeeiiirrdd as you want.

Compensation

All of our finances are fully transparent for the group, including all fees, expenses, costs, and payout for each performer. This includes both tips and pay (yes, each performer is equally paid)

Selection Process

Applications are reviewed by our curation team. Selected performers will be contacted within 2-3 weeks. We vet each application to ensure you fit within a marginalized community and align with our values.
